# Stryde Loyalty Programme

**Welcome to Stryde Rewards — the more you move, the more you earn.**

---

## 1. How It Works

The Stryde Loyalty Programme rewards you every time you shop. Earn points on every purchase, unlock higher tiers, and enjoy exclusive benefits.

**Earning Points:**
- Earn **1 point for every ₹10 spent** on eligible products
- Points are credited to your account within **24 hours** of order delivery
- Points are not earned on shipping charges, taxes, or gift card purchases

**Redeeming Points:**
- **100 points = ₹10 discount**
- Minimum redemption: 500 points (= ₹50 discount) per order
- Maximum redemption: 5,000 points (= ₹500 discount) per order
- Points can be redeemed at checkout — enter the amount you wish to redeem
- Points can be combined with a single discount code

---

## 2. Loyalty Tiers

Your tier is determined by your **total number of completed orders** (returned orders do not count).

| Tier | Orders Required | Points Multiplier | Free Express Shipping | Early Sale Access | Exclusive Products |
|------|----------------|-------------------|----------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Bronze | 0–4 orders | 1x | No | No | No |
| Silver | 5–10 orders | 1.5x | No | Yes (24hr early) | No |
| Gold | 11+ orders | 2x | Yes (unlimited) | Yes (48hr early) | Yes |

**Points Multiplier Example:**  
A Silver member spending ₹2,000 earns 300 points (200 base × 1.5x multiplier).  
A Gold member spending ₹2,000 earns 400 points (200 base × 2x multiplier).

## 5. Points Expiry

- Points expire after **12 months of account inactivity** (no purchases made)
- Making any purchase resets the 12-month inactivity clock
- Points earned from referrals expire if the referred friend returns their first order
- Bonus points from activities (reviews, social) expire after 6 months if unused

---

## 6. Tier Maintenance

- Tiers are calculated on a **rolling 12-month basis**
- Your tier is reviewed every month
- If your order count drops below the threshold in the past 12 months, your tier is adjusted the following month
- Example: If you reached Gold with 11 orders but only placed 4 orders in the past 12 months, you return to Bronze tier

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I transfer points to another account?**  
No. Points are non-transferable and linked to your individual account.

**What happens to my points if I return an order?**  
Points earned from a returned order are deducted from your balance upon return approval.

**Do I keep my tier if I return an order?**  
Returned orders are not counted toward your order total for tier calculation.

**Can I redeem points on sale items?**  
Yes. Points can be redeemed on any order, including items on sale.

**How do I check my points balance?**  
Log in to your account and visit "Loyalty Points" under your profile, or see your balance displayed at checkout.

**I didn't receive points for my order. What do I do?**  
Points are credited within 24 hours of delivery. If they haven't appeared after 48 hours, email support@stryde.in with your order number.
