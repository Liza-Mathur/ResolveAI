# Stryde Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** October 1, 2024

---

## 1. Shipping Destinations

Stryde currently ships across **India only**, covering 20,000+ pin codes. Enter your pin code on the product page to confirm delivery availability at your location. International shipping is planned for 2025.

---

## 2. Shipping Charges

| Order Value | Shipping Charge |
|-------------|----------------|
| Above ₹999 | FREE |
| ₹500 – ₹999 | ₹79 flat |
| Below ₹500 | ₹99 flat |

Express delivery is available at an **additional ₹99** for eligible pin codes.

---

## 3. Delivery Timeframes

| Delivery Type | Timeframe | Availability |
|---------------|-----------|--------------|
| Standard Delivery | 5–7 business days | All serviceable pin codes |
| Express Delivery | 2–3 business days | Metro cities and select tier-2 cities |
| Same Day Delivery | By 9 PM if ordered before 11 AM | Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai only |

Business days exclude Sundays and national public holidays. During peak sale periods (like End of Season Sales), delivery may take 1–2 extra business days.

---

## 4. Order Processing

- Orders placed before **2 PM IST** on a business day are typically dispatched the same day.
- Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends/holidays are dispatched the next business day.
- Custom or pre-order items may have extended processing times listed on their product page.
- You will receive a dispatch confirmation email with a tracking link once your order leaves our warehouse.

---

## 5. Order Tracking

- A tracking link is sent via **SMS and email** upon dispatch.
- Track your order anytime under **My Orders** in your Stryde account.
- Stryde ships via partner couriers including Blue Dart, Delhivery, Ekart, and Xpressbees, depending on your location.
- Live tracking is available 4–6 hours after the tracking number is generated.

---

## 6. Delivery Attempts

- Our courier partner will attempt delivery **up to 3 times**.
- After 3 failed attempts, the package is returned to our warehouse.
- If your order is returned due to failed delivery or incorrect address, we will contact you to reship. Reshipping charges apply.
- Ensure your delivery address and contact number are correct at the time of ordering.

---

## 7. Signature and Proof of Delivery

- Most deliveries require a signature or OTP confirmation upon receipt.
- If you are unavailable, a neighbour or family member may accept the delivery on your behalf.
- A delivery photo is taken by the courier as proof of delivery and is available in the tracking link.

---

## 8. Damaged Packages at Delivery

- If your package appears visibly damaged at the time of delivery, **do not accept it** and note the damage with the delivery agent.
- Alternatively, accept the package but record a video of yourself opening it.
- Report damaged packages within **48 hours** to support@stryde.in with photos or video evidence.

---

## 9. Lost Shipments

If your tracking status shows no movement for more than **3 business days**, please contact us:

- Email: support@stryde.in
- Phone: 1800-STRYDE-1

We will file a trace with the courier. If the shipment is confirmed lost, we will dispatch a replacement or issue a full refund within 5 business days of investigation completion.

---

## 10. Special Delivery Situations

**Bulk Orders (10+ items):** May be shipped in multiple packages. All packages will share a single order number and separate tracking IDs.

**PO Boxes:** We do not ship to PO Boxes. A full street address is required.

**Remote Areas:** Some remote pin codes may have extended delivery times of up to 10–12 business days. This is communicated on the product page and checkout.

---
