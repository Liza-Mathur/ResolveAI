# Stryde Warranty Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** October 1, 2024

---

## 1. Overview

Stryde stands behind the quality of its products. This Warranty Policy covers manufacturing defects for eligible product categories. It does not cover damage resulting from normal wear and tear, misuse, accidents, or unauthorised modifications.

---

## 2. Warranty Coverage by Category

| Product Category | Warranty Period | Covered |
|-----------------|-----------------|---------|
| Footwear | 6 months from purchase | Manufacturing defects: sole separation, stitching failure, midsole collapse under normal use |
| Electronics (Watches, Earbuds) | 12 months from purchase | Manufacturing defects, battery defects, sensor malfunction |
| Equipment (Foam Rollers, Resistance Bands, Massage Guns) | 6 months from purchase | Structural defects, motor failure (massage gun) |
| Apparel & Accessories | Not covered by warranty | Covered by 30-day return policy instead |

---

## 3. What Is Covered

The warranty covers defects that are the result of **manufacturing faults**, including:

- Sole separation or delamination on footwear (without signs of outdoor abuse)
- Stitching or seam failure under normal use
- Electronic malfunction not caused by physical damage or water ingress beyond rated resistance
- Structural failure of equipment under normal recommended use
- Battery defects causing significantly reduced performance within the warranty period

---

## 4. What Is NOT Covered

The warranty does **not** cover:

- Normal wear and tear (outsole wear, fabric pilling, colour fading)
- Damage caused by accidents, misuse, or neglect
- Damage from improper washing or care (e.g., machine washing footwear)
- Water damage beyond the product's rated water resistance
- Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality (scratches, dents)
- Products purchased from unauthorised resellers
- Products with removed or tampered serial numbers
- Damage caused by using the product for purposes other than its intended use

---

## 5. How to Make a Warranty Claim

**Step 1:** Email **warranty@stryde.in** with the subject line: "Warranty Claim – [Order Number]"

**Step 2:** Include the following in your email:
- Full name and registered email address
- Order number and date of purchase
- Product name and product ID
- Clear description of the defect
- Photos or video clearly showing the defect

**Step 3:** Our warranty team will review your claim within **3 business days** and respond with next steps.

**Step 4:** If the claim is approved, we will arrange a free pickup of the defective item.

**Step 5:** Upon receipt and inspection, we will either:
- Send a **replacement product** (same product, same size/colour or closest available)
- Issue a **full refund** to your original payment method if a replacement is not available

---

## 6. Warranty Claim Resolution Timeframe

| Stage | Timeframe |
|-------|-----------|
| Initial review and response | 3 business days |
| Pickup of defective item | 3–5 business days after approval |
| Inspection at warehouse | 2–3 business days after receipt |
| Replacement dispatch or refund | Within 2 business days of inspection |

---

## 7. Proof of Purchase

A valid proof of purchase (Stryde order number or invoice) is required for all warranty claims. Warranty claims without proof of purchase will not be accepted.

---

## 8. Warranty Transfer

Stryde warranties are **non-transferable** and apply only to the original purchaser. Products purchased as gifts are covered under the original purchaser's warranty, provided proof of purchase is available.

---

## 9. Limitation of Liability

Stryde's liability under this warranty is limited to repair, replacement, or refund of the defective product. Stryde is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from product defects.

---

## 10. Consumer Rights

This warranty policy does not affect your statutory rights as a consumer under applicable Indian consumer protection laws. If you believe your statutory rights exceed the coverage provided here, please contact us to discuss your specific situation.
