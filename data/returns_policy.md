# Stryde Returns & Refunds Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** October 1, 2024

---

## 1. Return Window

Stryde offers a **30-day return window** from the date of delivery for most products. To be eligible for a return, your item must meet all conditions listed in Section 3.

---

## 2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are **not eligible for return** under any circumstances:

- Innerwear, underwear, and socks
- Face masks and hygiene products
- Items marked as **Final Sale** or **Non-Returnable** on the product page
- Gift cards and digital products
- Any item that has been used, washed, altered, or damaged by the customer
- Footwear with visible signs of outdoor use (dirty outsoles, scratches)

---

## 3. Return Conditions

For a return to be accepted, the following conditions must be met:

- Item must be **unused and unwashed**
- All **original tags must be attached**
- Item must be in **original packaging**
- Footwear must show no signs of outdoor use — trial on carpeted surfaces only
- Return must be initiated within **30 days of delivery date**
- Original proof of purchase (order number) must be provided

---

## 4. How to Initiate a Return

**Step 1:** Log in to your Stryde account at stryde.in  
**Step 2:** Navigate to **My Orders** and select the relevant order  
**Step 3:** Click **Return Item** and select the item(s) you wish to return  
**Step 4:** Select your return reason from the dropdown menu  
**Step 5:** Choose between **Refund** or **Exchange**  
**Step 6:** Schedule a pickup — our courier partner will collect within 3–5 business days  
**Step 7:** Ensure the item is packed securely in its original packaging when the courier arrives

You will receive a confirmation email with a return tracking number once the pickup is scheduled.

---

## 5. Refund Processing

Once your returned item is received at our warehouse:

- **Quality inspection:** 2–3 business days
- **Refund initiation:** Within 24 hours of inspection approval
- **Refund credit timeline:**
  - Credit/Debit Card: 5–7 business days
  - UPI / Net Banking: 1–3 business days
  - COD orders: Refund via NEFT to your bank account within 5–7 business days (bank details required)
  - Stryde Store Credit: Instant upon inspection approval

Stryde Store Credit refunds carry a **5% bonus** — for example, a ₹1,000 refund becomes ₹1,050 in store credit.

---

## 6. Exchanges

Exchanges are available for:
- A **different size** of the same product
- A **different colour** of the same product (same price point)

Exchanges for a completely different product are not available — please initiate a return and place a new order.

Exchange delivery follows standard shipping timelines (5–7 business days). Express exchange (2–3 business days) is available at ₹99 for Gold loyalty members.

---

## 7. Damaged, Defective, or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item:

- Email **support@stryde.in** within **48 hours of delivery**
- Include your **order number** and **photos of the item** clearly showing the issue
- Stryde will arrange an **immediate replacement** or **full refund** at no cost to you, including return shipping
- No return shipping label is required for defective or wrong item cases — we will arrange pickup

This policy applies regardless of whether the item falls within the standard return window.

---

## 8. Return Shipping

- **Customer-initiated returns** (size, preference, change of mind): Return shipping is free for Gold loyalty tier members. Silver and Bronze tier members are charged a flat ₹79 return pickup fee, deducted from the refund.
- **Defective/wrong items:** Stryde covers all return shipping costs.
- **Self-ship option:** Customers may choose to self-ship returns to our warehouse. Stryde is not responsible for items lost or damaged during self-ship returns. Use a tracked courier service and email the tracking number to support@stryde.in.

---

## 9. Warranty Claims

Warranty claims are handled separately from the standard returns process:

- **Footwear:** 6-month manufacturing defect warranty from purchase date
- **Electronics (watches, earbuds):** 12-month manufacturing defect warranty
- Warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear
- For warranty claims, email **warranty@stryde.in** with order number, photos, and description of the defect
