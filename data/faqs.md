# Stryde Sports – Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Ordering & Payment

**Q: What payment methods does Stryde accept?**
A: Stryde accepts all major payment methods including Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Debit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders under ₹5,000.

**Q: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?**
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Please contact our support team immediately at support@stryde.in or call 1800-STRYDE-1.

**Q: Is it safe to save my card details on Stryde?**
A: Yes. Stryde uses PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways. We do not store your full card number on our servers. All transactions are encrypted with 256-bit SSL.

**Q: I was charged but didn't receive an order confirmation. What should I do?**
A: Please wait 15 minutes as confirmation emails can be delayed. Check your spam folder. If you still haven't received it, contact support@stryde.in with your payment reference number and we will resolve it within 2 hours.

**Q: Can I use multiple discount codes on one order?**
A: Only one discount code can be applied per order. Loyalty points and a single discount code can be combined, however.

**Q: Does Stryde offer EMI options?**
A: Yes. EMI is available on Credit Cards from HDFC, ICICI, Axis, and SBI for orders above ₹3,000. EMI options are displayed at checkout.

## Shipping & Delivery

**Q: How long does standard delivery take?**
A: Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days across India. Express delivery (2–3 business days) is available for most metro cities at an additional charge of ₹99.

**Q: Does Stryde ship internationally?**
A: Currently Stryde ships within India only. International shipping is on our roadmap for 2025.

**Q: How much does shipping cost?**
A: Shipping is free on all orders above ₹999. Orders below ₹999 attract a flat shipping fee of ₹79.

**Q: How can I track my order?**
A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also track your order under "My Orders" in your Stryde account.

**Q: My order shows delivered but I haven't received it. What do I do?**
A: First check with family members or neighbours who may have accepted it. Check the delivery photo in the tracking link. If the issue persists, contact us within 48 hours of the marked delivery time at support@stryde.in.

**Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?**
A: Delivery address can be changed within 1 hour of placing the order. After that, it cannot be changed as the order is already being processed.

**Q: Does Stryde deliver to rural or remote areas?**
A: We deliver to 20,000+ pin codes across India. Enter your pin code on the product page to check availability at your location.

## Returns & Refunds

**Q: What is Stryde's return policy?**
A: Stryde offers a 30-day return window for most products. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags intact. Footwear must be tried only on carpeted surfaces indoors.

**Q: How do I initiate a return?**
A: Log in to your account, go to "My Orders", select the order and click "Return Item". Choose the reason, schedule a pickup, and our courier will collect the item within 3–5 business days.

**Q: Are there any items that cannot be returned?**
A: The following cannot be returned: innerwear, socks, face masks, items marked as Final Sale, and any item that has been used, washed, or damaged by the customer.

**Q: When will I receive my refund?**
A: Once your return is received and inspected (2–3 business days), the refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. UPI refunds are typically faster (1–2 days).

**Q: Can I exchange a product instead of returning it?**
A: Yes. During the return process, select "Exchange" instead of "Refund". You can exchange for a different size or colour of the same product, subject to availability.

**Q: I received a damaged or wrong item. What do I do?**
A: We're sorry! Please email support@stryde.in with your order number and a photo of the item within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange an immediate replacement or full refund at no extra cost.

## Sizing & Fit

**Q: How do I find the right size?**
A: Each product page has a detailed size guide with measurements in cm. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to the guide rather than going by your usual size, as sizing can vary across product categories.

**Q: Stryde shoes feel tight — should I size up?**
A: Running shoes should have about a thumb's width of space at the toe box. If you're between sizes, we recommend going up half a size for running shoes. For training and weightlifting shoes, go true to size.

**Q: Do Stryde tights and shorts run true to size?**
A: Generally yes. If you're between sizes and prefer a tighter compression fit, go down. If you prefer room to move, go up. Our size guide has waist and hip measurements for reference.

**Q: Do Stryde products shrink after washing?**
A: Stryde activewear is pre-shrunk and made from synthetic fabrics that resist shrinking. Wash in cold water on a gentle cycle and avoid tumble drying to maintain the best fit and fabric quality.

## Products & Stock

**Q: A product I want is out of stock. Will it be restocked?**
A: Most products are restocked regularly. Click "Notify Me" on the product page to get an email alert when it's back in stock. Seasonal and limited edition items may not be restocked.

**Q: Are Stryde products ethically made?**
A: Yes. Stryde works with manufacturing partners who are GOTS and SA8000 certified. We publish our annual sustainability report on our website.

**Q: Do Stryde products come with a warranty?**
A: Footwear carries a 6-month manufacturing defect warranty. Electronics (watches, earbuds) carry a 12-month warranty. Apparel and accessories do not carry a warranty but are covered by our return policy.

**Q: How do I care for my Stryde footwear?**
A: Remove insoles and laces before washing. Hand wash with mild detergent and cold water. Air dry away from direct sunlight. Do not machine wash or tumble dry footwear as this can damage the cushioning and adhesives.

## Loyalty Programme

**Q: What is the Stryde Loyalty Programme?**
A: The Stryde Loyalty Programme rewards you with points for every purchase. 1 point is earned per ₹10 spent. Points can be redeemed as a discount on future orders (100 points = ₹10 discount).

**Q: What are the loyalty tiers?**
A: There are three tiers — Bronze (0–4 orders), Silver (5–10 orders), Gold (11+ orders). Higher tiers get faster shipping, early access to sales, exclusive products, and bonus points on purchases.

**Q: Do loyalty points expire?**
A: Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity. As long as you make at least one purchase per year, your points will remain active.

**Q: How do I check my points balance?**
A: Log in to your Stryde account and go to "Loyalty Points" under your profile. You can also see your points balance at checkout before placing an order.

**Q: Can I transfer loyalty points to someone else?**
A: Points are non-transferable and linked to your individual account only.

## Account & Technical

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and you'll receive a reset link within 2 minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

**Q: Can I have multiple addresses saved on my account?**
A: Yes. You can save up to 5 delivery addresses on your account under "Manage Addresses" in your profile settings.

**Q: How do I delete my Stryde account?**
A: Go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Note that deleting your account will permanently remove all order history and loyalty points. This action cannot be undone.

**Q: The Stryde app is crashing. What should I do?**
A: Try force closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the latest version from the App Store or Play Store. Contact support@stryde.in if the problem continues.
